Miles Apart, Hearts Together ✈️

Distance is just a test to see how far love can travel...


I know it’s not always easy, Kaali. Being in a long-distance relationship means we spend more time on screens than in each other’s arms. It means counting down days, looking at old texts, and wishing I could just reach out and hold your hand.

We might not have thousands of photos together yet, but every single memory we do have is etched into my heart. I cherish the sound of your voice over a late-night call more than anything else in the world.

"Distance teaches us to appreciate the days we are together and defines our love as something that doesn't need physical presence to grow."

The miles between us are just temporary. One day, the 'goodbye' at the airport will become a 'goodnight' in our own home. Until then, remember that no matter how many kilometers separate us, you are the closest person to my heart.

I love you through every mile. 💗
